Enviroment Causes
Garbage

The United States has a big solid waste problem. Citizens produce more than four pounds of trash per person each day, and landfills are nearing capacity. Research and write an essay describing the causes of the growing garbage problem and the effects on the environment. Provide evidence to support your findings.
Climate

Is it getting hotter each summer? Research information on climate change in the last century. Write an essay describing the changes that have happened since 1970 to present day and explain what caused the changes. Provide evidence to support your explanations.
Pollution

Chemical pollutants can ruin natural resources such as water and air. Research types of chemical pollution. Then, write an essay about the causes of chemical pollution and the effects it has on our natural resources. Provide examples and evidence to support your response.
